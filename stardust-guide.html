How to get enough Stardust for Cassiopeia and Draco Constellations

🔥 How To Get Enough Stardust for Cassiopeia and Draco Constellations

  1. World 1 locked runes (Slime Rune 2nd locked rune x20 Stardust, Fire Rune 2nd locked rune x20 Stardust, Water Rune both locked runes x12 Stardust and x20 Stardust)
  2. Higher rarity before doing stardust gives higher stardust production (Investing lava in gen 1 can help with this)
  3. World 2 locked runes (Snow Rune 1st locked rune x20 Stardust)
