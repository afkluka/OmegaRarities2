🔥 Challenge 5 Guide

  1. Go to options, turn on last setting (True RNG)
  2. Find the closest rune to 1/25k but slightly above
  3. Changing or upgrading passive, completing clover asc’s and plasma milestones are the easiest adjustments to help you get closer to 1/25k if your odds are too far above 1/25k

📌 Notes

  1. Within step 3, you have to try different combinations of all the suggested ways of altering your odds e.g. re entering the challenge to reset clover ascensions and testing with different passives, then try doing plasma milestones with different passives etc etc
  2. If you are lazy or can’t figure out the guide, afk 1m event rune for the 1/200k which should take on average roughly 4 hours, however a rune closer to 1/25k can be up to 8x faster
  3. Due to differences in RPS and Rune clone, the rune needed to hit 1/25k will be different for most people which is why this guide was created
